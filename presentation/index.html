💰 Sales History

A paginated, filterable history of all recorded sales. View transaction details, share receipts as images, and manage sales with edit/delete actions.

  • Date range filtering (From → To)
  • Payment method chips: Cash, Transfer, Credit
  • Itemized sale details with subtotal/tax/total
  • Long-press to share receipt as PNG
  • Delete with stock restoration
  • Cursor-based pagination (50 per page)

🏦 Finance Tracker

Track all income and expenses — both automatic from sales and manual entries. Monitor cash flow with category-based expense tracking.

  • Income & Expenses tabs with monthly totals
  • Manual income entry (title + amount)
  • Expense categories: Stock, Rent, Utilities, Transport, Other
  • Edit/delete with undo via toast
  • Auto-recorded stock purchase costs
  • Server-side date filtering

📦 Inventory Management

Manage products and services with real-time stock tracking, pricing, and product images. Quick-adjust stock levels and protect products with sales history.

  • Inventory value at sell & buy prices
  • Debounced search with server-side LIKE query
  • Low-stock filter and warning badges
  • Quick +/- stock adjustment
  • Product image picker (1:1, 0.7 quality)
  • Services with infinity stock badge
  • Delete protection for products with sales history
